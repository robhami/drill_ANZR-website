Services

Drilling Optimization

We provide drilling-data analysis and optimization roadmaps that identify opportunities to reduce Non-Productive-Time (NPT) and improve Rate of Penetration (ROP).

Optimization is based on:

  1. Identifying where ROP can be improved by assessing and addressing mechanical and hydraulic inefficiencies within the drilling system.
  2. Identifying where rig operations can be safely streamlined in terms of duration and personnel/equipment efficiency.

These roadmaps are supported by an industry-leading analytical process with visual outputs of the data analysis to educate and guide users on where efficiencies can be achieved.

Complimentary services include:

  1. Log interpretation (Geophysical and Vibrational data)
  2. Data plotting and analysis
  3. Dull product evaluation and recommendations
  4. Pre-job system optimization (Hydraulics, Mechanical Efficiencies)
